### Reductionism vs Holism Debate
Emergence challenges strict reductionist approaches.

#### Strong Emergence
```
Micro-level laws + Initial conditions â†’ Novel macro-level laws
```

- **Irreducibility**: Macro properties not predictable from micro alone
- **Downward Causation**: Higher levels influencing lower levels
- **Multiple Realizability**: Same macro properties from different micro bases
- **Context Dependence**: Properties emerging only in specific contexts

#### Weak Emergence
```
Micro-level laws â†’ Predictable macro-level patterns
```

- **Computational Irreducibility**: Patterns requiring simulation to predict
- **Effective Theories**: Approximate descriptions at different scales
- **Coarse-Graining**: Averaging micro details to reveal macro patterns
- **Approximate Reduction**: Macro laws derivable with approximations
